Overview

This blog post generator is a tool that helps you generate content using machine learning. The blog post generators take your inputs (topic, keyword research, etc.) and act as a content brief generator of your ideas. Then, it uses most up-to-date python framework to generate blog ideas based on what users had inputted.

The blog generator website can perform the following actions.

  1. Submit blog details including (name of blogger, title of blogs, subtitle of blogs and summary of the blog)
  2. View details of information submitted
  3. Access blog details submitted
  4. Read other exciting blog stories

Our blog website hopes to provide users with the most exciting features while browsing through different blog stories.
